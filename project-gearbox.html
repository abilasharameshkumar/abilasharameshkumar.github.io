Project Overview

This personal project involved creating a complete SolidWorks CAD model of a planetary gearbox. A planetary gear system uses a central sun gear, multiple planet gears rotating around it, and an outer ring gear, providing high torque multiplication in a compact form factor.

The project was driven by a desire to practise modelling complex mechanical assemblies, including precise gear tooth profiles, mating constraints, and motion simulation. It demonstrates advanced SolidWorks skills and a solid understanding of mechanical power transmission.
