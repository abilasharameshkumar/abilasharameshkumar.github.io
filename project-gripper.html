Two-Finger Robotic Gripper

A two-finger robotic gripper designed for precise grasping and torque/force analysis in mechanical applications. Built a lightweight, efficient gripper capable of securely grasping 200g objects.

Project Overview

Developed a functional two-finger robotic gripper through concept design, CAD modelling in SolidWorks, and detailed force/torque analysis. The project focused on both mechanical design and performance optimisation, including evaluating grip strength and stability under varying loads.

Iterative design improvements led to a lightweight yet robust prototype, showcasing the ability to translate engineering theory into a working practical solution.

Analysis and Design
  • Designed and prototyped a two-finger robotic gripper using a four-bar linkage system with servo actuation
  • Conducted force/torque and kinematic analyses to optimise link geometry, gear synchronisation, and actuator selection
  • Used SolidWorks for iterative CAD modelling and MATLAB for motion/force simulations and design validation
  • Built a lightweight, efficient gripper capable of securely grasping 200g objects
