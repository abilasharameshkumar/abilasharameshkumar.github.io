Project overview

Developed a functional two-finger robotic gripper through concept design, CAD modelling in SolidWorks, and detailed force/torque analysis. The project emphasised both mechanical design and performance optimisation, including evaluating grip strength and stability under varying loads.

Iterative design improvements led to a lightweight yet robust prototype, showcasing skills in problem-solving, mechanical analysis, and translating engineering principles into a practical solution.

Analysis & design
  • Designed and prototyped a two-finger robotic gripper using a four-bar linkage system with servo actuation
  • Conducted force/torque and kinematic analyses to optimise link geometry, gear synchronisation, and actuator selection
  • Utilised SolidWorks for iterative CAD modelling and MATLAB for motion/force simulations and design validation
  • Built a lightweight, efficient gripper capable of securely grasping 200g objects, demonstrating integration of theory with practical engineering design
